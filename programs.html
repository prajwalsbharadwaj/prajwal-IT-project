Our Programs

Programs We Offer

Bachelor of Engineering

Our Bachelor of Engineering in Information Technology (B.E. in IT) program is designed to equip students with a comprehensive understanding of the theoretical and practical aspects of IT. This program focuses on the latest technologies, software development, database management, networking, and cybersecurity. With a blend of classroom learning, hands-on labs, and industry projects, students will be prepared to tackle real-world challenges in the IT industry. Graduates of this program will be well-equipped to pursue careers as software engineers, system analysts, network administrators, and IT consultants.

Evening College Program

Our Evening College Programs are tailored for working professionals and students who wish to further their education without compromising their daytime responsibilities. These programs offer the same rigorous curriculum and high standards as our daytime courses but are scheduled in the evening to provide flexibility.

Post Graduate Courses

Our Post Graduate Courses are designed for individuals who wish to deepen their expertise and advance their careers in the technology sector. We offer a range of specialized programs that cover cutting-edge technologies and advanced methodologies in the field.

Our Facilities

We offer state-of-the-art facilities to provide our students with the best learning environment and support their overall well-being.

World Class Library

Our library is stocked with a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources. It offers a quiet and comfortable space for students to study and conduct research, with access to the latest publications in various fields.

Largest Playground

Our expansive playground provides students with ample space for outdoor sports and recreational activities. It's equipped with modern facilities for basketball, football, athletics, and more, promoting physical fitness and teamwork.

Tasty and Healthy Food

Our cafeteria offers a variety of delicious and nutritious meals, catering to diverse dietary preferences. We ensure that students have access to healthy food options to support their well-being and academic performance.
